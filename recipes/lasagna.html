Lasagna

Description

This is a recipe for making a classic Lasagna dish to be served for a group!

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Lasagna pasta
  • Onion and Garlic (optional)
  • Tomato sauce
  • Spices and seasoning of your choice
  • Cheeses (Ricotta, String, etc.)

Steps

  1. Season and cook the Ground Beef
  2. Prepare and heat the Tomato Sauce
  3. Begin boiling the Lasagna pasta until soft
  4. Drain pasta once done and begin layering the pasta, ground beef, cheese, and tomato sauce within a sizable baking tray
  5. Once done, place the baking tray in the oven and leave on 200 to warm to let the dish simmer
  6. Monitor dish over time, take out once done
